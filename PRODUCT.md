# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Primary users are two equal-priority groups:
- Internal operators (owner and staff) managing cashier, transactions, queue, bookings, stock, analytics, and configuration.
- Public customers using landing, order, booking, and payment-instruction surfaces.

They use the system in fast-paced, real-time contexts where clarity, speed, and confidence are critical.

## Product Purpose
This product is an end-to-end POS and operations platform for small restaurant/warung businesses, covering customer-facing ordering and internal operational workflows in one system.

Success means:
- Operators can complete core tasks quickly with low error risk.
- Public customers can place orders/bookings with minimal confusion.
- Information stays consistent across operational and public surfaces.

## Brand Personality
- Clear
- Trustworthy
- Efficient

Tone should be practical and confident, never playful for its own sake. The interface should feel stable under pressure and easy to scan at a glance.

## Anti-references
- Overly playful or decorative UI patterns that distract from operational tasks.
- Visually noisy interfaces that reduce focus and increase cognitive load.
- Styling choices that prioritize novelty over legibility and speed.

## Design Principles
1. Prioritize task completion speed over decorative complexity.
2. Keep information hierarchy explicit so critical actions are always obvious.
3. Use consistent interaction patterns across internal and public flows.
4. Design for high-confidence operation in busy, time-sensitive contexts.
5. Reduce ambiguity in labels, states, and confirmations.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Baseline target: WCAG AA.
- Ensure readable contrast for all primary text and controls.
- Preserve usability for mixed device contexts (desktop operator workflows and mobile public flows).
- Keep motion purposeful and non-essential, with reduced-motion-friendly behavior where applicable.
